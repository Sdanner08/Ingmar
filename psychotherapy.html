Psychotherapy

I seek to aid individuals in their capacity for growth, while attending to their strengths, to develop confidence and balance in their personal and professional lives.

Psychotherapy can help Third Culture Kids of all ages by examining how a highly geographically mobile upbringing has impacted psychological development. Focuses may include challenges concerning of identity, restlessness, cultural homelessness. Therapy may also aid in cultivating the benefits of such an upbringing.

Harm Reduction Psychotherapy is a treatment integrating Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Psychodynamic Therapy, and Mindfulness approaches. Particular focus is attended to the goals of the individual, whether it be abstaining or moderating their substance use.

Psychedelic Integration Therapy is designed to assist those seeking support in connection with psychedelic experiences. It is tailored to meet individuals with full engagement and open curiosity.
